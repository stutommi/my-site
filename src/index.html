Music

Short Bio

I grew up most of my childhood in Kainuu. I started getting interested about guitar playing seriously when I was around 13 years old. I've played and studied music from year to year ever since. I finished my latest studies when I graduated from Metropolia (UAS) in 2018.

Currently I'm gigging and teaching mainly in and around Helsinki area.
