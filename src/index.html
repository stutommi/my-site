Music

Short Bio

Growing up in Kainuu I started getting interested about guitar playing seriously when I was around 13 years old. I've played and studied music ever since. I finished my latest studies when I graduated from Metropolia (UAS) in 2018.

Currently I'm gigging and teaching mainly in and around Helsinki area.

Guitar Lessons

As a professionally educated Guitar teacher, I'm happy to offer lessons to anyone who's interested in learning this great instrument!

Where?

For private lessons, I'll give lessons in students homes (or other place that feels suitable) on condition that it's around Helsinki area. For reference, it's fine for me to travel from Munkkiniemi to Katajanokka.

I also teach at Rytmimusiikkiopisto Tauko in Espoo. It's a great place to study music in small groups and I warmly recommend it to everybody!

For who?

I've taught music and specially guitar playing around 8 years now. That includes people aged from 6 to over 50. I'm also open to teaching people with disabilites (e.g Down syndrome / Autism).

No prior experience?

No problem! I love teaching people who have just started on their guitar journey just as much as those who's been doodling with them for some time!

Not sure?

If you're are not sure whether guitar playing lessons are for you (or someone you know), no problem: We can have one or two test lessons and if you feel the lessons are not for you, I don't charge anything for them!

Web Development

Short History

(let experience = 0; experience < lifetime; experience++)

It started at my friends place on fall 2018. We had no idea what coding was about and I started googling about it's actual meaning. My friend found codecademy and we played with it for a while.

After that I decided that I would try to learn some basics of it to see if I could build anything useful out of it. I started with javascript course on codecademy. I found out I enjoyed the logic and the challenge it holds. After completing it I was hungry for more so I started codecademys web development path which is a little bigger piece to handle (basics in HTML, CSS, JS, Jquery, MySQL, Github, React, Node, Express).

Codecademys lessons are managed into nice bite-sized pieces and they are rewarding and fun to do. At the same time I was longing to make something useful with the skills I learned. We started a funny and challenging (ongoing) project with my brother. It is an automated plant-watering system, there's some info about it in projects section.

After finishing codecademys web development path I've been learning mainly with different tutorials, own projects, docs, Stack Overflow and Google. When I'll see some awesome way to present something one the web I'll strive to recreate it myself. My motivation to develop my skills have only grown since the start!

info

This app is running on my raspberry PI and is an collaboration between me and my brother. My brother is in charge of sensors and labview programs, which serve the values online. My app then fetches those values, displays them and stores them in a database. Right now it is giving it's user information about current humidity and temperature of different rooms and plants. It also gives information about the history of these values in chart forms. Our goal is to get the app water the plants based on the humidity values given by the sensor.

This is an ongoing project, and the site with the code given is constantly changing!
